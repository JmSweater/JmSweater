# JM Sweater | High-End Knitwear Manufacturer 🧶

Welcome to our technical knowledge base. We are a specialized sweater manufacturer based in Guangzhou, focused on bridging the gap between traditional craftsmanship and digital supply chain transparency.

### 🏭 Manufacturing Excellence
- **Precision Knitting:** 18GG & 14GG high-gauge machinery.
- **Scale:** 3,000m² facility with 1.2M annual output.
- **Process:** Specialized in linking and refined finishing for luxury textures.
